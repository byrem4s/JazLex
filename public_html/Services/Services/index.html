Electrical/Mechanical Drafting Service

We understand every solution or project requires updated documentations to either support on-site execution or as part of overall project documentation. Our team of trained drafters are prepared to support your drawing need whether it is in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, or another native format.

Harmonic Study Services

We follow the standard harmonic guidelines outlined in IEEE 519 and will perform both stages of testing: Simulation studies using software such as ETAP and On-site testing using your Single Line diagram and PCC (Point of Common Coupling).

Power Factor Study service

Non-unity Power factor at your site will impact your electrical bill and add penalties from the Grid provider. Therefore, it is crucial to ensure you have close to unity power factor at site. Addition of Inductance or Capacitance at site will change your overall line impedance and will contribute to your power factory analysis. Our team is ready to support your needs to either bring up your system to electrical standard.
