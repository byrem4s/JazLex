Trusted Expertise in Electrical Reliability and Modernization

Jazlex Consulting is an Ontario-based engineering consultancy specializing in asset management, reliability, modernization strategy, and lifecycle optimization of industrial equipment in the heavy industries. With over 15 years of leadership experience across multiple industries and applications, we help public and private sector clients reduce unplanned downtime, improve asset performance, and future-proof critical infrastructure.

Our team is led by licensed Professional Engineers (P.Eng) and PMP-certified project managers that bring together deep technical knowledge, strategic business insight, and global execution experience across industries. Whether you're managing aging medium/low-voltage systems, planning capital upgrades, or implementing predictive maintenance programs — we help you take action with confidence.

Our Mission

To empower municipalities, OEMs, and industrial operators, specifically end users who directly deal with onsite equipment with practical, high-impact solutions that extend the life, performance, and efficiency of their electrical and control systems.

Our Values

  • Reliability: Our work improves the reliability of your systems and operations
  • Practicality: We deliver recommendations that work in the real world
