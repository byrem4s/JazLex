Engineering Power Studies

Any changes at site may impact the overall power system infrastructure; therefore, every change requires an impact study to be performed. Common components such as Transformers, reactors, Capacitance, and motors may change the infrastructure and change the power factor or tuning harmonic frequencies. Typically, impact studies are performed in two stages: Simulation and On-site. Our team can help support you in your journey whether you need to help during design stage, impact studies, or implementation stage.
